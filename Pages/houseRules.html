House Rules



Complying with these House Rules at the Vacation Rental Property is a requirement of the vacation rental agreement. Failure to adhere to the below House Rules may result in penalties such as deductions from security deposit.

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS


All guests must comply with all House Rules and any other instructions from the Property Manager during their stay. Guests must notify the Property Manager of any disputes or complaints as soon as possible. No visitors, fireworks, animals allowed. No flowers or plants to be removed.

NOISE AND THE NEIGHBORHOOD


Guests are fully responsible for the safety and security of their children at all times, as well as any disturbance caused to other residents in the neighborhood. Guests must keep noise to a minimum as not to disturb the occupants of neighboring properties â€“ especially during nighttime hours (eg. 22h00-08h00) and upon arrival and departure. Excessive noise is prohibited at all times and may result in termination of the vacation rental agreement, eviction, loss of rental paid and extra charges which may be deducted form the security deposit.

FUNCTIONS


Parties and large gatherings of any kind are strictly prohibited at the Vacation Rental. Any small gathering must comply with other rules set regarding noise and the neighborhood.

SECURITY


Any time guests leave the Vacation Rental, it is their responsibility to ensure all windows and doors are closed/locked. Guests must switch off lights, fans, electronics such as TV's when not in use to promote energy saving. Excessive use of power will result in deduction of security deposit.

SMOKING


No smoking of cigarettes/ hubbly bubbly or vape/e-cigarettes permitted inside the Vacation Rental. Cigarette butts must be disposed of properly in ashtrays provided and not thrown on property grounds. Hubbly bubbly coals must be completely dead before discarding, Failure to do so will result in a deduction from the security deposit.

DAMAGES AND BREAKAGES


All damages and breakages must be reported to the Property Manager as soon as possible. No furniture is to be moved and/or from one room to another without prior agreement.

HYGIENE


If you need help our maid is available at a set fee per day which will be arranged. Poor housekeeping and unhygienic living conditions is not acceptable.

CHECK-OUT ARRANGEMENTS


Check-out is strictly 10h00 am in order to prepare for the guests arriving 15h00.

COMPLIANCE


Breach of any of these house rules is a breach of the terms and conditions of occupancy as per the rental agreement. The owner and manager reserve the right to terminate permissions to occupy and to evict from the Vacation Rental, any guest who refuse to follow these House Rules or who cause a nuisance to neighbors or other residents of the community. Curfew from 22h00-6h00 unless otherwise
