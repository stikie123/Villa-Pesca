About us

Situated in MeerEnSee, Richards Bay, Villa Pesca is a spacious semi-self-catering home for up to 8 people.

The house has 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, a kitchen, a dining area and an outdoor living area. Three bedrooms have a king-size bed that can be converted into 2 single beds and an en-suite bathroom with a shower. The fourth bedroom has a double bed with an en-suite bathroom fitted with a shower. Guests must please bring their own towels.

The large kitchen has a dining area for 8 people and is equipped with a stove, a microwave, a fridge, and tea- and coffee-making facilities. A TV with Netflix and Wi-Fi access is available.

The living area opens onto a large stoep with an undercover swimming pool, and overlooks the boma and BBQ area.

Deep-sea fishing can be arranged on request at an additional cost, and Villa Pesca is situated 1 km from various shops and restaurants and 2 km from Alkantstrand beach for fishing and swimming. Meer En See offers a variety of activities such as golfing, and bird watching, and guests can also take the Dune Route to explore the surroundings.
